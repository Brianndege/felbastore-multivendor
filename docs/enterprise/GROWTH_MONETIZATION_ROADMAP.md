# Growth Playbook, Launch Strategy, and Monetization Roadmap

## Growth Playbook

- Referral engine: double-sided rewards + milestone bonuses + anti-fraud checks
- Influencer/affiliate marketplace: tracked links, cohorts, payout automation
- Ambassador program: challenge quests, leaderboard, UGC incentives
- Social proof automation: verified purchase prompts + testimonial collection
- Viral launch loops: waitlist, countdown pages, share incentives, press kit

## Traffic Maximization Add-ons

- Content marketing blog with topical SEO clusters
- Programmatic SEO landing pages for category/location intents
- Community layer for collections, follows, and public wishlists
- Built-in share card generator and deep-link distribution

## Monetization Roadmap

1. Core: transaction fees + vendor subscriptions
2. Expansion: sponsored products + featured listings + premium users
3. Marketplace ads: self-serve ad bidding and targeting
4. Premium formats: flash sales, limited drops, auctions
5. Enterprise: SLA plans, dedicated support, custom integrations

## Launch Strategy

- Wave 1: checkout reliability + vendor onboarding + analytics baseline
- Wave 2: referral and affiliate loops + SEO + campaign automation
- Wave 3: AI recommendations/fraud/churn + multi-region growth
